Everything You Need to Know

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method where you study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After a few rounds, you take a longer break. It helps you stay focused and avoid burnout.

Which study method should I start with?

If you’re new, begin with Pomodoro + Active Recall. This combo helps you focus and remember more. You can add other methods later as you figure out what works best for you.

How long should my study sessions be?

It depends on your focus level. Most people start with 25-minute sessions, but you can adjust to 30, 45, or even 60 minutes as you get better at concentrating.

Do these methods work for any subject?

Yes. Whether you're studying programming, math, languages, or school subjects, these techniques improve focus and memory across all topics.
